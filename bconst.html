The b Constant


The b constant is a constant of proportionality called Wein's displacement constant.

It is equal to:

b = 2.8977729(17)×10^−3 meters⋅Kelvin


However, if you want to find the answer in micrometers, you can use:

b ≈ 2900 micrometers⋅Kelvin
