The history of Wilhelm Wien


Wilhelm Wien was the man who used theories about heat and electromagnetism in 1893 to deduce Wien's law.
Wien received the 1911 Nobel Prize for discovering the law
He was born 13 January 1864 in Gaffken (Modern Poland)
In 1866, his family moved to Dachstein (Modern Austria)
